All longstanding contributors to Rolling Stone's record review section,..... Broadly speaking, their assignments were based on decades: McGee handles the artists of the Fities and earlier; Evans tackled the Sixties; Coleman concentrated on the Seventies; and Considine covered from the Eighties to the present. Beyond that chronological breakdown, each writer took on subjects outside their focus either to make a point about certain artists and their relation to a particular period styles - or for the sheer fun of it.
